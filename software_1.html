Project Overview

Migrated and developed content management system, transitioning documentation from Astoria to Cosima platforms. Developed and maintained a report system for maintenance documents, ensuring seamless access and accuracy for technical users.

The Challenge

Structural Differences: Managed strict rules and validation requirements due to differing structure restrictions between Astoria and Cosima, ensuring data integrity throughout the migration.

Graphics Conversion: Engineered automated graphics conversion to maintain consistency between legacy and new systems, preserving all links and references.

Reporting Consistency: Guaranteed that reporting results from both the former and new systems were identical, supporting reliable documentation and compliance.

Our Approach

Automated Migration: Automated the conversion of one database format to another, enforcing strict data rules and schemas, which reduced manual effort and minimized errors.

Efficiency Gains: Accelerated graphics conversion and migration processes by 50%, significantly reducing the time required for manual transfers.

Consistent Reporting: Delivered a consistent, reliable process for generating outcome reports, ensuring all stakeholders receive accurate and up-to-date documentation.

Impact & Results

Managed a CMS with approximately 20,000 active documents for one department, 600 CBDOC, 30 topics, and 60 pages. Supported additional documentation sets (SKB, SPC) and integrated with SAP PEAK as needed.
