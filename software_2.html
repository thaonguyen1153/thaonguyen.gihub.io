Project Overview

Designed and implemented of the Component Maintenance Manual (CMM) system, centralizing and modernizing maintenance documentation for aircraft components. The system supports a wide range of document types (PDF, HTML, WORD), including CMMs, Fault Isolation Manuals, Temporary Revisions, Service Bulletins, and Service Information Letters.

Included technologies and tools: VizEx Transform (Larson Technology), CGM, SVG, SGML, XML

Addressed and resolved challenges

Interactive Visualization: Enabled interactive and detailed visualization of component parts, improving clarity and usability for maintenance personnel.

Standards Conversion: Managed the complex conversion of documentation between ATA iSpec 2200 and S1000D standards, ensuring compliance across all documentation.

Document Diversity: Supported a wide variety of document types and formats, streamlining access and management for all stakeholders.

Outcomes Achieved

The CMM process involves receiving defective aircraft parts for repair or replacement, with approved materials and work steps defined in the manual, providing direct feedback and clear instructions for repair workers.

Centralizing the process eliminated time-consuming phone calls and inquiries due to unclear details, ensuring a streamlined and efficient workflow.
