Project Overview

Designed and developed the corporate website for Tanner Vietnam, creating a modern, responsive online presence that showcases services, projects, and industry expertise to clients in both English and Vietnamese markets.

Implemented clear information architecture and navigation to make it easy for prospects and partners to find relevant content about solutions, case studies, and contact channels.

Applied technologies and tools: WordPress, HTML5, CSS3, responsive design frameworks, on-page SEO optimization, and basic analytics integration (e.g., tracking visits and key user flows).

The Challenge

Brand and content alignment: Translated existing offline and fragmented digital materials into a cohesive website structure, ensuring that branding, tone, and visual identity were consistent across all pages. (Align with corporate website at Tanner Deutschland)

Multi-language readiness: Structured content and templates so the site could support bilingual content (English and Vietnamese) without duplicating effort, enabling scalable updates.

Performance and usability: Optimized page layouts, media assets, and front end code to improve load times and provide a smooth experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

Our Approach

Cohesive site structure ensuring consistent branding & visual identity

Built scalable templates for easy English/Vietnamese updates

Define and standardize media breakpoints and assets to meet responsive mobile needs.

Impact & Results

Professional online presence: Delivered a responsive, client-facing website that improved Tanner Vietnam’s credibility and visibility with international customers and partners.

Content maintainability: Built reusable page templates and configured the WordPress admin to allow non-technical staff to update newsletters, job posting, text, images, and blog posts without developer support.

Improved discoverability: Applied basic SEO best practices (metadata, headings, clean URLs) to support better search engine visibility and easier discovery of key service offerings.
